A virus has brought your city's operations to a standstill. Experts predict that some patterns may be able to break the virus spread and allow mobility. However, they need people's help to find these patterns.


You have 30 days left to come up with a novel strategy to save your city. Use your resources wisely to create good patterns and break the virus chain.

×

Goal: Grow green patches in 30 days.

Rules:

  • Get 3 or 4 greens together for a green patch.
  • Use 1,2 greens to connect patches.
  • Try to avoid big patches.

Actions:

  • Click or tap to select grey patch.
  • Double click or tap to Undo
  • Press spacebar or Press Forward to turn green

Hint: Try your own creative pattern(s).
