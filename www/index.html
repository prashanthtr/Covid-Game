A pandemic has brought your city's operations to a grinding halt. As people are finding livelihood difficult, we have to open up the city whilst risking more infections.


Thorugh careful assessment, experts have come up with patterns of disease spread. However, they need more people to look at these patterns and find ways to break the virus chain. It is time for citizens to step up.


You have 30 days left to come up a novel strategy to save your city. Use your resources wisely to assemble safe zones and enable people find their livelihood in safety.

×

Instructions for gameplay.

  • Single click -> Intervention
  • Double click -> Undo intervention
  • Spacebar -> Move forward to next day in the game

Patterns of disease spread.

  • 4 or more nearby green -> grey
  • 1 or less nearby green -> grey
  • 2,3 or 4 nearby green -> green
  • Intervention on grey -> green

Green: Safe ; Grey: Infected
